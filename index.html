About Me

A calm voice in complex moments

I'm Mark Collett, a mediator with deep roots in conflict resolution, people development and human behaviour. My career spans private mediation practice, running a national mediation firm, senior leadership in the corporate world and now directing ADR Technology Group, a technology-driven Alternative Dispute Resolution company.

I hold a Masters degree from the University of Stellenbosch, focused entirely on alternative dispute resolution, as well as a Bachelor of Business Administration in HR and Financial Management and a BA Honours in Social Science.

My experience spans mediating civil, family and workplace disputes referred by courts, law firms and direct clients. I also bring extensive coaching and mentoring experience from corporate environments across South Africa and internationally, alongside a rare blend of formal ADR training, strategic leadership and genuine empathy for the people I work with.

Services

Mediation for every context

Conflict takes different forms, and each calls for a tailored approach. My two core practice areas address the most common disputes with methods proven to produce durable, consensual outcomes.

Family Mediation

Divorce and separation are among life's most difficult transitions. I help families negotiate the road ahead, from parenting plans to asset division, in a way that preserves dignity and protects children's wellbeing.

  • Divorce & separation mediation
  • Parenting plans & co-parenting
  • Elder care & inheritance disputes
  • Extended family conflict

Workplace Mediation

Unresolved tension in the workplace drains productivity, morale and talent. Workplace mediation tackles conflict early, before it escalates into formal grievances, disciplinary processes or tribunal claims.

  • Employee & manager conflicts
  • Team & inter-departmental disputes
  • Redundancy & restructure tensions
  • Return-to-work support
